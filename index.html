A little about me

My name is Collin Porter. I've lived in Utah all my life and don't have any plans to leave. It has everything I need. I currently work in the Behavioral Health industry and while it has been very rewarding, I am currently pursuing a career in the tech industry. I'm working on becoming a full-stack developer and hope to find a job in the field upon graduation.

Snowboarding at Brighton

Skate or Die

When I was 10 yrs old I picked up my first skateboard. For the past 20 years I have been skating and I've been hurt too many times to count. Shoulder surgeries, wrist and ankle fractures and so much more! I love skating more than most things in this world and I'll be doing kickflips when I'm 60 (hopefully). It's the one thing that has kept me sane over and over again.
